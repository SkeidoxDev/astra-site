Astra Bot - Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 03/01/2025

1. Introduction

Astra Bot (the "Bot") is a Discord bot designed to provide moderation, economy, and entertainment features. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle user data and ensure compliance with Discord's Terms of Service and Community Guidelines.

2. Data We Collect

Astra Bot collects minimal data necessary for its operation. This includes:

  • Server IDs and channel IDs (for moderation and logging features).
  • User IDs (for command usage tracking, economy system, and moderation purposes).
  • Command inputs and bot interaction data (to improve functionality and prevent abuse).

We do not collect: personal messages, email addresses, IP addresses, or sensitive personal data.

3. How We Use Data

The data collected by Astra Bot is used strictly for:

  • Providing bot features such as economy, moderation, and utility commands.
  • Ensuring user safety and preventing misuse of the bot.
  • Debugging and improving bot performance.

We do not sell or share user data with third parties.

4. Data Storage & Security

Astra Bot stores only necessary data and follows these security practices:

  • Data is stored securely with restricted access.
  • Regularly cleared temporary logs and cached data.
  • No storage of unnecessary personal data.

5. User Rights & Data Removal

Users have the right to request data removal. To delete your data:

  • Use the bot command `!deletedata` (if available).
  • Contact the Astra Team via our support server to request data removal.

Requests will be processed within a reasonable time.

6. Third-Party Services

Astra Bot may interact with Discord’s API and third-party libraries to provide its features. We are not responsible for data handling by these services.

7. Changes to the Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated. Any major changes will be announced in our support server. Continued use of the bot after updates implies acceptance of the revised policy.

8. Contact & Support

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us through our support server.

9. Data Retention Policy

Astra Bot only retains data for as long as necessary to provide its services effectively. We follow these data retention rules:

  • Temporary logs (e.g., command usage data) are automatically cleared within **30 days**.
  • Moderation records (e.g., warnings, bans) may be stored for up to **90 days** unless manually removed.
  • Economy-related data (e.g., virtual currency, inventory) remains **until the user requests deletion**.

Users can request immediate data deletion via our support server.

10. Children's Privacy

Astra Bot is intended for use by Discord users who comply with **Discord’s minimum age requirement** (typically **13 years old**, or **16 in some regions**). We do not knowingly collect data from children under this age.

If you believe that Astra Bot has collected data from a minor, please contact us immediately to request its removal.

11. Automated Decision-Making

Astra Bot may use automated decision-making for:

  • **Moderation actions** (e.g., automatic mutes for excessive spam).
  • **Economy system** (e.g., random event rewards, gambling outcomes).
  • **Bot performance improvements** (e.g., detecting abuse or misuse).

Users can appeal automated decisions by contacting the Astra Team in our support server.

12. GDPR & CCPA Compliance

Astra Bot follows international data protection regulations, including:

  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) (for EU users) – Users have the right to request, modify, or delete their data.
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) – Users may request details on what data is collected and request its deletion.

To submit a **GDPR or CCPA data request**, please contact us in our support server.

13. Limitation of Liability

By using Astra Bot, you acknowledge that:

  • The Astra Team is **not responsible for data loss, bot downtime, or third-party actions** affecting bot performance.
  • Bot usage is at your own risk, and **we make no guarantees** about uninterrupted service.
  • We are **not liable for damages** caused by user misuse of Astra Bot.

14. Final Agreement

By using Astra Bot, you agree to this Privacy Policy and understand that we may update it as needed. If you continue to use Astra Bot after any changes, it means you accept the revised policy.
